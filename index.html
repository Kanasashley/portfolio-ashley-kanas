a UI designer passionate about design

Creating something aesthetically pleasing excites me. UI design combines my love for understanding people’s needs with my passion for creating beautiful, functional designs. I have UX/UI skills that help me define user needs and translate them into intuitive, production-ready interfaces. I specialize in visual design and branding and have a foundational understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I strive to bring my clients’ ideas to life.

My work

Crave

Crave is a location-based web app that mimics social media interfaces, appealing to younger users who want to discover and share dining experiences. During the design process, I employed UX skills such as user research and testing to ensure a user-centered approach. I developed user flows and wireframes to create an intuitive experience and crafted mockups to bring the interface to life with a friendly, engaging UI.

Remi

Remi is a native productivity app designed to boost a user’s productivity through positive reinforcement. Remi, the mascot, helps users stay motivated and productive by offering encouraging feedback and celebrating achievements. This project demonstrates my skills in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and visual design.
