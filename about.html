About me

My story

Hi! I’m a UI designer located in the Hudson Valley. I’m passionate about creating visually engaging and easy-to-use digital experiences. I have a solid foundation in design essentials like wireframing, user research, lean UX, user psychology, user personas, responsive web design, branding, iconography, style guides, animation, and high-fidelity mockups. My goal is always to blend beauty with functionality.

With experience in baking and volunteering as an educator, I’ve built up skills in time management, attention to detail, problem-solving, communication, and empathy. My interest in interior design has also helped me understand color and aesthetics more deeply.

I’m skilled in Figma and GitHub and have a good working knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which helps me create designs that are easy for developers to work with. My focus is on visual design and branding, aiming to build clear, memorable brand identities. I’m excited to bring fresh ideas and a thoughtful approach to each project!
