About

My story

Hello! I’m a UI designer passionate about creating visually engaging and user-friendly digital experiences. I recently completed a comprehensive UI design course at CareerFoundry, where I built a strong foundation in various design concepts, including wireframing, user research, lean UX, user testing, user psychology, user personas, responsive web design, branding, iconography, style guides, animation, and high-fidelity mockups.

Although I'm new to the field, I’m eager to learn and grow every day. My diverse background has equipped me with valuable hard and soft skills: from my baking experience, I gained expertise in visual design, attention to detail, time management, and problem-solving. Volunteering as an educator sharpens my communication, empathy, public speaking, and feedback skills. My personal interest in interior design enhanced my understanding of color theory and aesthetics.

I am proficient in Figma and GitHub, and I completed a secondary course in Frontend Development for Designers with CareerFoundry, where I gained knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This technical understanding enhances my design work by enabling me to create more practical and technically feasible designs, ensuring smoother collaboration with developers and a more seamless user experience.

I thrive on the challenge of understanding users' needs and translating them into visually appealing and functional designs. Specializing in visual design and branding, I focus on creating cohesive and compelling brand identities that leave a lasting impression. I’m excited to bring fresh ideas and a keen eye for visual design to every project I undertake.
